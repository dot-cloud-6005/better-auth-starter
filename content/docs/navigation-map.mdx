The navigation map displays location data for equipment and plant items, helping track assets geographically.

## Viewing the map
1. Go to **Navigation Map** from the main menu.
2. The interactive map shows equipment markers.
3. Click markers to see details and status.

## Map controls
- **Zoom**: Use mouse wheel or plus/minus buttons
- **Pan**: Click and drag
- **Trailing**: Click locate me or star icon to follow your coordinate
- **Layer toggle**: Switch between coloured marks or just amber (coloured marks reference the asset colour)

## Searching assets
Use the search bar to show specific:
- Asset id's
